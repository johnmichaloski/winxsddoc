Attribute Group: AttrMeasuredPoint

Name AttrMeasuredPoint
Documentation The AttrMeasuredPoint attributeGroup provides attributes for the combined uncertainty and mean error of measured 3D points and vectors. Uncertainty and/or error may be specified that applies to all three values (X, Y and Z) and/or uncertainty and/or error may be specified individually for X, Y, and Z. If both types of accuracy are given, the individual values should be used, not the group value.
